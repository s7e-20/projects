NICK DRAKE TABS

Hello, welcome to nickdraketabs.co.uk!

This is a very simple site set up purely to hold all the Nick Drake tabs I've worked out over the years. These are the same tabs as have appeared for a long time on Mikael Ledin's excellent The Nick Drake Files - but as Mikael no longer wishes to update that site, nickdraketabs.co.uk lets me keep the tabs up to date with additions or corrections that I might discover or which people might send me.

You can contact me at chrishealey[aatt]ndirect.co.uk, replacing [aatt] with @. Please write Nick Drake in the subject line or I may miss your mail amongst the waves of spam.

The tabs are of course provided for free - I hope you enjoy playing them as much as I do.

- Chris Healey
